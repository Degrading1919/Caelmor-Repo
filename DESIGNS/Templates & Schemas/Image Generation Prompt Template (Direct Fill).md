## Negative Prompt (if supported)
- No text, no logos, no watermarks
- No exaggerated fantasy armor, no glowing runes, no spectacle magic VFX (unless explicitly required by canon)
- No extreme lighting, no cinematic fog, no busy background
- No anime/chibi proportions; keep realistic grounded proportions
- Avoid resemblance to recognizable third-party IP designs

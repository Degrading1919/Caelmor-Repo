## A) OUTPUT CLASS CATALOG

### SMITHING

**Primary weapon frameworks**  
- **Primary Skill:** Smithing  
- **Secondary Skills Enabled:** Fabrication, Adornment  
- **Primary Player Motivation:** Mastery  
- **Gameplay Purpose:** Establishes a combat identity and determines how force is delivered and controlled in melee.  
- **Risk/Reward Tie:** Enables higher-risk engagements by improving reliability under pressure.  
- **Resource Roles Consumed:** Structural, Conductive, Stabilizing  
- **Dependency Note:** Requires Mining for progression and Fabrication for finalization.  
- **If Missing Consequence:** Combat progression caps early and feels blunt and shallow.  
- **Tier:** Early mastery  

**Tool-grade metal instruments**  
- **Primary Skill:** Smithing  
- **Secondary Skills Enabled:** All gathering skills  
- **Primary Player Motivation:** Efficiency  
- **Gameplay Purpose:** Improves reliability and access in gathering loops without directly increasing combat power.  
- **Risk/Reward Tie:** Allows players to operate longer in hostile zones with fewer failures.  
- **Resource Roles Consumed:** Structural, Stabilizing  
- **Dependency Note:** Depends on gathering skills to justify demand.  
- **If Missing Consequence:** High-risk gathering becomes frustrating and inconsistent.  
- **Tier:** Early mastery  

**Structural combat reinforcements**  
- **Primary Skill:** Smithing  
- **Secondary Skills Enabled:** Adornment  
- **Primary Player Motivation:** Mastery  
- **Gameplay Purpose:** Alters how damage is mitigated and recovered from during prolonged fights.  
- **Risk/Reward Tie:** Reduces punishment for mistakes while increasing repair and upkeep pressure.  
- **Resource Roles Consumed:** Structural, Stabilizing  
- **Dependency Note:** Requires Adornment to realize specialization.  
- **If Missing Consequence:** Survivability paths feel binary instead of tunable.  
- **Tier:** Mid mastery  

**Environmental breach tools**  
- **Primary Skill:** Smithing  
- **Secondary Skills Enabled:** Mechanisms  
- **Primary Player Motivation:** Discovery  
- **Gameplay Purpose:** Opens new routes, shortcuts, or sealed world spaces.  
- **Risk/Reward Tie:** Enables access to dangerous areas earlier than intended.  
- **Resource Roles Consumed:** Structural, Conductive  
- **Dependency Note:** Mechanisms needed to contextualize use.  
- **If Missing Consequence:** Exploration becomes flat and overly gated.  
- **Tier:** Mid mastery

## B) TIER-OF-MASTERY PLACEMENT

- Early mastery: foundational access, reliability, endurance  
- Mid mastery: specialization, environmental control, cross-skill synergy  
- High mastery: identity lock-in, system-bridging, high-risk optimization
